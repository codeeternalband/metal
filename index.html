Biography

Code Eternal has a fresh and straight-forward take on progressive metal. The band was founded in South Ostrobothnia, Finland in 2021. In 2022, they released their instrumental debut EP "Sixth Wave", which showcased their technical maturity and compositional ambition — at a time when most members were just 15 years old. Now joined by powerful vocalist Teemu Dahlman, the band is preparing its first studio album, promising to push the boundaries of progressive metal.
